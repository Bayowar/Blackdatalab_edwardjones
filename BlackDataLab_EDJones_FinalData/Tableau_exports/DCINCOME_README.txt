DC INCOME DATA (1960-2020)
============================================================

FILES:
1. dc_income_distributions_1960_2020.csv - Complete income bracket data
2. dc_income_metrics_1960_2020.csv - Calculated income distribution metrics
3. dc_income_metadata.json - Complete documentation and methodology

KEY FEATURES:
- Covers 1960, 1970, 1980, 1990, 2000, 2010, 2020
- Income data from Census, NHGIS, and ACS
- Inflation-adjusted to 2020 dollars for comparison
- Standardized 4-category income brackets

ACADEMIC NOTES:
- 1960: FAMILY income (different unit from later years)
- 1970-2000: HOUSEHOLD income from NHGIS
- 2010-2020: HOUSEHOLD income from ACS
- Use percentages and inflation-adjusted values for comparisons
